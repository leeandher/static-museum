JACKET

Do you enjoy hurting people?


Jacket is the protagonist from the 'Hotline Miami' franchise. You could argue either way, that he's a serial killer, or that he's a vigilante hero, but regardless, his methods are brutal and effective.

After recieving cryptic messages on his answering machines, Jacket dons various animal masks and hunts down russian mobsters in their own hideouts. His disregard for subtlty comes from his past life as a US Army Soldier and, in the following game, generates a sadistic following of animal-masked killers.

BIKER

You're dead meat.

Biker is the second playable character from the first 'Hotline Miami' game. He is also a brutal assassin targeting the Russian Mobs throught Miami. Aware of his violent actions, Biker insists on completing his missions because he's "bored".

After recieving the same cryptic messages on his answering machine, Biker goes to trace down the phone numbers and is met with a veiled threat from the callers. Confronting the threat, Biker kills his way into their hideout and learns the truth of the 50 Blessings organization.
